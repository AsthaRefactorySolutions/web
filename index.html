Who We Are?

Aastha Refractories is a leading manufacturer, exporter, and supplier of high-quality Refactory products catering to industries such as Steel, Cement, Glass, and Petrochemicals. Our product range includes High Alumina Bricks, Fire Clay Bricks, Sillimanite Bricks, Insulation Bricks, Bottom Pouring Sets, Basic Refractories, Acid-Proof Refractories, Monolithic Refractories (Mortars, Castables, Ramming Masses, and Gunning Masses), and Refactory Bed Materials. We also specialize in custom Refactory shapes tailored to client-specific requirements. Our advanced facility is equipped with cutting-edge grinding sections, heavy-duty friction screw presses, high-temperature tunnel kilns, and a fully equipped quality control laboratory. Rigorous testing ensures superior quality and performance, including Pyrometric Cone Equivalent (PCE), Refractoriness Under Load (RUL), Cold Crushing Strength (CCS), and chemical analysis. With a focus on precision engineering and client satisfaction, Aastha Refractories delivers reliable and durable solutions that exceed industry standards.
