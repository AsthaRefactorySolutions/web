Fiber Board


Fiber Board, offered by Aastha Refractory Solutions, is a versatile, high-quality insulation material designed for superior thermal and acoustic performance. Manufactured using a blend of organic and inorganic fibers, this product delivers excellent thermal stability, soundproofing capabilities, and durability. Lightweight and easy to install, Fiber Board is ideal for applications in construction, automotive, and manufacturing industries. Available in two temperature grades—RT-1100°C (2012°F) and HTZ-1260°C (2300°F)—it ensures reliable performance in high-demand, heat-intensive environments. Its eco-friendly nature aligns with modern sustainability standards, making it a long-lasting and efficient insulation solution.

Quality of Fiber Board

  • High-performance thermal insulation to regulate temperature in various applications.
  • Superior soundproofing qualities for noise reduction in industrial and commercial spaces.
  • Durable and lightweight design for easy handling and installation.
  • Eco-friendly composition, ensuring minimal environmental impact.
  • Excellent resistance to moisture and temperature fluctuations, ensuring longevity in demanding environments.

Applications of Fiber Board

  • Thermal insulation in walls, ceilings, and floors for residential, commercial, and industrial buildings.
  • Acoustic insulation in soundproof rooms, offices, and studios.
  • Thermal protection for high-temperature equipment in factories and manufacturing plants.
  • Back-up insulation for HVAC systems, reducing energy loss and improving efficiency.
  • Sound barriers in automotive industries to reduce noise within vehicles.
