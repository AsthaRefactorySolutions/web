Glass Wool


Glasswool, available at Aastha Refactory Solutions, offers excellent insulation for both thermal and acoustic applications. It's lightweight, durable, and provides superior fire resistance and chemical stability. This versatile material is used in buildings, HVAC ducts, and ceilings to regulate temperature and reduce noise. Glasswool also resists biological growth, ensuring longevity in various environments. Additionally, it is available in different forms, such as rolls and slabs, with various facings like aluminum foil and glass tissue, making it adaptable for different insulation needs.

Quality of Glass Wool

  • Thermal powerhouse, keeps temperatures steady and reduces energy bills.
  • Sound barrier, absorbs noise to create a peaceful and quiet environment.
  • Fireproof to protect both people and property in high-risk areas.
  • Flexible and lightweight, making it simple to work with and install.
  • Eco-friendly, crafted from recycled glass, helping you reduce your carbon footprint.

Applications of Glass Wool

  • Home insulation, for better energy efficiency in residential buildings.
  • Commercial insulation, keeping businesses efficient and comfortable year-round.
  • Soundproofing in offices, music studios, and auditoriums for a quieter space.
  • Thermal insulation for industrial equipment, ensuring optimal performance in high-heat settings.
  • Piping and duct insulation, minimizing heat loss and enhancing HVAC efficiency.
