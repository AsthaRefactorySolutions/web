Lose Cerawool


Loose Cerawool, offered by Aastha Refractory Solutions, is a premium insulation material crafted from high-purity ceramic fibers to deliver exceptional thermal performance. Lightweight, non-combustible, and highly adaptable, it is ideal for filling gaps, cracks, and irregular spaces in high-temperature environments. With superior thermal stability, low thermal conductivity, and excellent flexibility, Loose Cerawool ensures efficient insulation for industrial applications. Available in temperature grades up to 1260°C (2300°F), it is perfect for furnaces, kilns, refractory linings, and other heat-intensive equipment. Trusted by Aastha Refractory Solutions, this eco-friendly material guarantees reliable, long-lasting performance for demanding thermal needs.

Quality of Loose Cerawool

  • Composed of high-purity ceramic fibers for outstanding thermal insulation.
  • Offers excellent thermal stability and resistance to high temperatures.
  • Lightweight, non-combustible, and eco-friendly material.
  • Low thermal conductivity and high energy efficiency.
  • Flexible and easy to use, adaptable for various shapes and spaces.

Applications of Loose Cerawool

  • Filling gaps and irregular spaces in high-temperature equipment like furnaces and kilns.
  • Insulation for Reftractory linings and complex industrial machinery.
  • Ideal for packing and sealing in high-temperature zones.
  • Acoustic insulation for reducing noise in industrial environments.
  • Used in backup insulation for boilers, ovens, and other heat-intensive equipment.
