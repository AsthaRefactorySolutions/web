Calcium Silicate Board


Calcium Silicate Boards from Aastha Refactory Solutions are an exceptional thermal insulation product capable of withstanding high temperatures. These boards are lightweight, durable, and fire-resistant, making them ideal for various industrial and construction applications. Made from a mix of Portland cement, fine silica, and selected fibers, they offer excellent dimensional stability even in damp and humid environments. Calcium Silicate Boards are perfect for insulating piping, equipment, and ceilings, as they maintain structural integrity even in harsh conditions. The material is unaffected by moisture, steam, sunlight, and fungal growth, ensuring long-lasting performance and reliability.

Quality of Calcium Silicate Board

  • High fire resistance, providing enhanced safety in high-temperature environments.
  • Excellent thermal insulation properties, minimizing heat transfer and improving energy efficiency.
  • Moisture and chemical resistance, ensuring durability in challenging environments.
  • Lightweight and easy to install, reducing labor costs and installation time.
  • Eco-friendly composition, aligning with modern sustainability standards.

Applications of Calcium Silicate Board

  • Thermal insulation for industrial equipment such as boilers, furnaces, and kilns.
  • Fireproof walls and partitions in buildings and commercial spaces.
  • Protective lining for high-temperature equipment in refineries, petrochemical plants, and power stations.
  • Thermal insulation in pipes and ducts to prevent heat loss and increase energy efficiency.
  • Acoustic insulation in sound-sensitive environments, providing noise reduction.
