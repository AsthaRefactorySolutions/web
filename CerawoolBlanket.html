Cerawool Blanket


The Cerawool Blanket, offered by Aastha Refractory Solutions, is a premium ceramic fiber insulation material designed for high-temperature applications such as kilns, furnaces, and other high-heat industrial processes. Manufactured from high-purity spun fibers, this lightweight and flexible insulation blanket delivers exceptional thermal resistance, low thermal conductivity, and low heat storage, ensuring superior energy efficiency. Its durability, combined with its resistance to chemical attacks, makes it a reliable solution for diverse thermal needs. Completely inorganic and eco-friendly, the Cerawool Blanket is easy to cut, install, and handle, further enhancing its usability in various settings. Available in two temperature grades—RT-1260°C (2300°F) and HTZ-1425°C (2600°F)—it meets the rigorous demands of high-temperature environments while maintaining long-lasting performance and structural integrity.

Quality of Cerawool Blanket

  • Made from high-purity spun ceramic fibers ensuring superior thermal resistance.
  • Lightweight, flexible, and durable for long-term performance.
  • Low thermal conductivity and heat storage, enhancing energy efficiency.
  • Resistant to chemical attacks, ensuring reliability in harsh environments.
  • Eco-friendly and easy to handle, cut, and install.

Applications of Cerawool Blanket

  • Insulation for furnaces, kilns, and high-temperature industrial equipment.
  • Lining for high-heat areas in refineries, petrochemical plants, and power stations.
  • Heat shields for fire protection and thermal barriers.
  • Acoustic insulation in noise-sensitive environments.
  • Back-up lining for Reftractory linings in furnaces and boilers.
